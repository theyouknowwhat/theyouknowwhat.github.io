Hello! Welcome to my Soon-To-Be Web Page!

Chances are you are here because you already know me.
We're probably friends! Maybe we met thru music or art or comics.
Or maybe we simply crossed paths somewhere on the Wild Road of Life.

Either way:

Welcome!

To the future site of my Personal Web Page.

This is something I have been thinking about for a very long time
-- to build a web site, to gather and share my art, and to stay connected with friends
free of the algorithms and consumerist surveillance of social media.

Inspired by my new friend Ana and her amazing website & newsletter, I finally started on my own earlier this year.

I'm still working on it but I hope to get it to a place where I can share it soon!
All hand-coded by me. There are no templates or site builders!
I feel that HTML & CSS are art forms in themselves & part of my idea for this page is to be a place for me to explore its possibilities!

Like a lot of people my age, I remember the internet as this special new thing. A beautiful and freaky deaky place where it seemed like anything could happen and new worlds were unfolding daily.
The current state of the World Wide Web, its transformation into a series of Platforms and Apps I think is a very sad fate!

Something truly beautiful is being gutted before our very eyes, not dissimilar to the myriad ways the natural, wild world is turned into parks and highways.
And how our cities & cultural scenes & undergrounds full of vibrant surging life are gutted and turned into ' shopping experiences ' and ' looks ' and ' styles ' devoid of content, ready for quick consumption.

But! It doesn't have to be that way.

The internet still has the potential it did in its utopian Early Days
& I hope this site can serve as my own small contribution
to making the internet interesting & beautiful & surprising & fun once again.
